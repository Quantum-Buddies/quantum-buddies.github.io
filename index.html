About Us

RYOUSHI: QUANTUM BUDDIES

Ryoushi is the primary startup brand: a compact quantum software studio focused on practical algorithms, clear systems thinking, and elegant product execution.

Quantum Buddies remains the research and community-facing initiative underneath that umbrella, where we explore hybrid quantum-classical ideas, hackathon submissions, and open-source prototypes.

The goal is to make advanced quantum work feel rigorous, credible, and visually distinctive — more like a modern quantum software company than a dense academic poster.
