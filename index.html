About Us

Quantum Buddies is a research-focused organization dedicated to advancing the field of quantum computing and its applications in machine learning, scientific discovery, and real-world problem solving.

Our team combines theoretical quantum computing knowledge with practical implementation skills, tackling complex challenges through innovative hybrid quantum-classical approaches. We actively participate in hackathons, research collaborations, and open-source development to push the boundaries of what's possible with quantum technologies.

From quantum attention mechanisms for scientific discovery to hybrid neural networks for medical diagnosis, our work spans multiple domains, always with a focus on practical applications and meaningful impact.
